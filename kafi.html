Raag Kafi



Raag Kafi is mostly performed on holi, its lyrics are also composed on holi.
It consists of 7 nodes both in aaroh and avroh
so, the nature of this raag in sampoorn-sampoorn.
Aaroh=C# D# E F# G# A# B (C# high optic)
Avroh= (C# hih optic) B A# G# F# E D# C#
It is suppose to be sung at midnight.
Vadi of this raag is G# and samvadi id C#.
This raag belongs to kafi thaat.
