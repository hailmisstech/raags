Raag Bhupali


Raag Bhupali is one of the most famous raag.
It contains 5 nodes in aaroh and avroh
so the nature of this raag is Audav-Audav
Aaroh = C# D# F G# A# (C# high optic)
Avroh = (C# high optic) A# G# F D# C#
It is suppose to be sung on the first watch of the night .
Vadi of this raag is E and Samvadi is A#.
This raag belongs to Kalyan thaat.
Rest nodes of this raag are anuvadi.

This raag has no vivadi nodes
